Publication and Indexing

Conference Proceedings

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings by Springer in the 'Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies' series (ISSN: 2190-3018).

Indexing

The proceedings will be indexed in major databases including:

  • Scopus
  • Web of Science
  • DBLP
  • Google Scholar
  • SpringerLink
  • INSPEC
  • WTI Frankfurt eG
  • zbMATH
  • SCImago

All books published in the series are submitted for consideration in the Web of Science.

Special Issues

Selected high-quality papers may be invited for extended versions in special issues of relevant journals. (To be announced)
