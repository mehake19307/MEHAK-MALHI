VISA Information

For the visa information, please visit Visitor Supporting Documents, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India. Please email for support icstais@cumail.in to request a visa invitation letter so that you can print and use in obtaining a visa. Obtaining a Visa Support Letter from ICSTAIS-2026 Conference will not guarantee that your India visa application will be approved. The letter is merely supplementary information that explains a visa applicant's intended purpose of travel to India.

Visa Support Letter Policy

For security purposes, the conference has policy for issuing official Visa Support Letters as follows:

  • Visa Support Letters will be issued only after you have successfully registered and paid for your conference.
  • Visa Support Letters can only be issued for the person registered to attend the conference in form of participant, author, chair and speaker.
  • Unfortunately we cannot issue visa Support Letters for guests of participants.

How to Request a Visa Support Letter

  1. Complete your registration and payment process for the conference.
  2. Email your request to icstais@cumail.in with the subject line "Visa Support Letter Request".
  3. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your full name as it appears on your passport
    • Your affiliation
    • Your registration confirmation number
    • Your paper ID (if you are an author)
    • Your nationality
    • The embassy/consulate where you will apply for your visa
  4. The conference organizers will process your request and send the visa support letter to your email within 5-7 working days.

Important Notes

  • Please apply for your visa well in advance of your travel date.
  • Processing times for visas vary by country and embassy.
  • The conference organizers are not responsible for visa application outcomes.
  • If your visa application is denied, the registration fee may be refunded according to our refund policy, minus administrative charges.
